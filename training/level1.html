Level 1 – The Pub Bartender

Goal: Ownership, leadership and training new team members.

✅ Checklist

  • Understand house rules and expectations
  • Pour beer and wine confidently
  • Serve spirit + mixer correctly
  • Know your main bar layout
  • Introduced to at least 3 classic cocktails
  • Complete Glassware & Station Standards module
  • Memorise all classic specs
  • Use correct shake/stir methods
  • Glassware and garnish standardised
  • Understand batching and prep logic
  • Can lead during moderate shifts
  • Trains and mentors new hires
  • Oversees prep and batch quality
  • Leads on shift setup and handovers
  • Deep cocktail theory: syrups, acids, modifiers
  • Can troubleshoot and support across bar ops
