Training – # Level 3

# Level 3

Goal:

✅ Checklist

  • All OBL drinks memorised
  • Understand all batching notes
  • Knows which drinks are shaken, stirred, or built
  • Can execute drink blindfolded (not recommended)
  • Knows the GP target for each cocktail (~80%)
  • Understands why ingredients are portioned exactly
  • Can flag if a spec causes GP issues
  • Recognises premium vs house spirits and upsell opportunities
  • Follows batch specs precisely
  • Dates all batches and keeps rotation tight
  • Can lead a prep session and assign tasks
  • Tracks waste and reports low stock
  • Trains Level 1s on bar setup
  • Coaches Level 2s on speed and spec accuracy
  • Maintains service calm during peak hours
  • Shares knowledge and lifts morale
  • Can work with Management on seasonal menus
  • Suggests new cocktails with GP in mind
  • Reads customer vibe and curates perfect drink
  • Applies historical knowledge and creativity in builds
  • All cocktail specs known
  • Can lead a full shift
  • Trusted by peers and supervisors
  • Ready for Level 4 (The Final Form)
