Training – # Level 2

# Level 2

Goal:

✅ Checklist

  • Memorise all OBL drink specs (Classics + House Riffs)
  • Shake, stir, and build with speed and accuracy
  • Use correct glassware, garnish, and ice without prompts
  • Maintain a clean and organised station
  • Begin supporting prep/batching processes
  • [Disco Sour](https://www.notion.so/Disco-Sour-207ce1fd2e9381d28503e5c1a26f3ecc?pvs=21)
  • [Fig Negroni](https://www.notion.so/Fig-Negroni-207ce1fd2e9381d4baebe94345a2c60a?pvs=21)
  • [Flint](https://www.notion.so/Flint-207ce1fd2e938196aaddf4c6b3865a33?pvs=21)
  • [Mezcal Paloma](https://www.notion.so/Mezcal-Paloma-207ce1fd2e9381ffa7f3f539080ef9b0?pvs=21)
  • [Spiced Sour](https://www.notion.so/Spiced-Sour-207ce1fd2e9381bc80bccd6b3782f704?pvs=21)
  • [Caipirinha](https://www.notion.so/The-Caipirinha-207ce1fd2e9381448d6fe9a4be70aaae?pvs=21)
  • [Pisco Sour](https://www.notion.so/The-Pisco-Sour-207ce1fd2e938169bec7c160c89807ab?pvs=21)
  • [Miso Butter Old Fashioned](https://www.notion.so/Miso-Butter-Old-Fashioned-207ce1fd2e9381239931dcce3254060e?pvs=21)
  • Correct shake type (reverse shake vs dry shake)
  • Consistent dilution when stirring
  • No overpouring – accuracy = GP control
  • Every drink served with appropriate garnish
  • Able to adjust for guest preferences (e.g. extra dry)
  • Read and follow batching specs
  • Use scales and labelled bottles
  • Date + label all prep items
  • Clean down after batching
  • Help Level 1 staff understand 3 key drinks
  • Provide guidance on ice/garnish/speed
  • Lead by example with consistent standards
