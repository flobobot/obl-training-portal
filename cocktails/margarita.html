  <h1>Margarita</h1>

  <h2>Ingredients:</h2>

  <ul>

    <li>50ml Ocho Blanco Tequila</li>

    <li>25ml Lime Juice</li>

    <li>15ml Triple Sec (Cointreau)</li>

    <li>10ml Sugar Syrup</li>

  </ul>


  <h2>Method:</h2>

  <p>Shake all ingredients with ice and fine strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Optionally, half-rim the glass with salt before pouring.</p>


  <h2>Pro Tip:</h2>

  <p>Salt rims aren’t mandatory—half-rimming offers guests choice without overpowering the drink. Always use fresh lime juice for best results.</p>


  <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

  <p>The Margarita likely evolved from the Daisy, a family of drinks made with citrus, sweetener, and spirit—"margarita" literally means "daisy" in Spanish.</p>
