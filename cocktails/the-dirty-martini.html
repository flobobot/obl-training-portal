Classic

The Dirty Martini

Classics

Ingredients

  • 60ml London Dry Gin
  • 10ml Dry Vermouth
  • 10ml Olive Brine
  • Green Olive (Garnish)

Method

Stir all ingredients with ice until well-chilled. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a green olive.

Pro Tip

Adjust the olive brine to taste. Too much can overpower the balance.

Did You Know?

The Dirty Martini dates back to the early 20th century and gained popularity in the mid-century cocktail boom.
