Classic

The Dirty Martini

Ingredients

  • East Ldn Gin or Vodka: 50ml
  • Dollin white vermouth: 10ml
  • Olive brine: 10ml

Method

Stir over ice and strain into a chilled martini glass.

Glassware

Nick & Nora

Garnish

Green olive

Pro Tip

Ask how dirty they want it — more brine = saltier, murkier.

Did You Know?

The Dirty Martini was created in 1901 when bartender John O’Connor added olive brine to a traditional Martini. How dirty can youn go? Beware of customers requesting ludicrous amounts of olives - this is not part of your five a day!
