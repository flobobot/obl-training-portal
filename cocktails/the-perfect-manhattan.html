Classic

The Perfect Manhattan

Ingredients

  • Bulleit Rye: 50ml
  • Cocchi Di Torino: 12.5ml
  • Dolin Dry Vermouth: 12.5ml
  • Angostura Bitters: 2 - 3 dashes

Method

Stir until properly diluted and silky. Julip strain into a chilled coupe.

Pro Tip

The “Perfect” Manhattan uses both sweet and dry vermouths for a layered profile. Stir carefully to avoid over-dilution. Pour from hight - it adds drama to the process but also aerates the mixture, making it much smoother.
