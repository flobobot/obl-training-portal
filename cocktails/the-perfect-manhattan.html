Classic

The Perfect Manhattan

Ingredients

  • Bulleit Rye: 50ml
  • Cocchi Di Torino: 12.5ml
  • Dolin Dry Vermouth: 12.5ml
  • Angostura Bitters: 2 - 3 dashes

Method

Stir until properly diluted and silky. Julip strain into a chilled coupe.

Glassware

Coupette

Garnish

Maraschino cherry, orange peel aromatised and discarded

Pro Tip

The “Perfect” Manhattan uses both sweet and dry vermouths for a layered profile. Stir carefully to avoid over-dilution. Pour from hight - it adds drama to the process but also aerates the mixture, making it much smoother.

Did You Know?

The Manhattan dates back to 1870s New York — its rye roots give it a spicy backbone loved by purists. It used to be my go-to just to get to the maraschino cherry as quickly as possible. Now I like to savor the moment and the three sips to get to the cherry. Sometimes two.
