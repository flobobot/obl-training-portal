Classic

The Margarita

Ingredients

  • 50ml Tequila (Blanco)
  • 25ml Lime Juice
  • 20ml Triple Sec (Cointreau)
  • Salt rim (optional)

Method

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Optionally rim the glass with salt before pouring. Garnish with a lime wheel.

Pro Tip

Use high-quality blanco tequila and freshly squeezed lime juice. The balance of sweet and sour is key.
