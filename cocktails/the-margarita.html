Classic

The Margarita

Ingredients

  • Ocho 8 Tequila: 50ml
  • Lime: 20ml
  • Agave Syrup: 5ml
  • Cointreau Triple Sec: 10ml

Method

Shake vigorously and fine strain into a coupette or rocks glass with optional salt rim.

Glassware

Coupette or Rocks

Garnish

Dehydrated lime wheel or salt rim

Pro Tip

Ocho’s peppery profile adds bright spice to the traditional Margarita.

Did You Know?

The Margarita’s origin is contested — some credit it to Tijuana in the 1930s, others to Texas in the 1940s. I just think it imply belongs in my mouth and can live in there rent free forever.
