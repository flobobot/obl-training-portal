Classic

The Caipirinha

Ingredients

  • Cachaça: 50ml
  • Brown sugar syrup: 15ml
  • Lime

Method

Muddle 4 lime wedges with sugar in a rocks glass, add crushed ice, pour cachaça, cap shake and serve.

Pro Tip

Use fresh limes and soft brown sugar for the most authentic flavour. Only use solid, thick glassware for this!
