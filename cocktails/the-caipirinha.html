Classic

The Caipirinha

Ingredients

  • Cachaça: 50ml
  • Brown sugar syrup: 15ml
  • Lime

Method

Muddle 4 lime wedges with sugar in a rocks glass, add crushed ice, pour cachaça, cap shake and serve.

Glassware

Rocks

Garnish

None — rustic serve

Pro Tip

Use fresh limes and soft brown sugar for the most authentic flavour. Only use solid, thick glassware for this!

Did You Know?

The national drink of Brazil, Caipirinha’s name comes from ‘caipira’, meaning a person from the countryside. It is, simply put, something that encapsulates long tradition and simplicity. The cap shake makes it very fun to make.
