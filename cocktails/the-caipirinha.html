The Caipirinha

Ingredients

  • 50ml Cachaça
  • 1 Lime (cut into wedges)
  • 2 teaspoons Brown Sugar

Method

Muddle lime wedges and sugar in a rocks glass. Fill the glass with crushed ice and pour over cachaça. Stir well to combine.

Pro Tip

Use fresh limes and avoid over-muddling the pith to prevent bitterness.

Did You Know?

The Caipirinha is the national cocktail of Brazil and is traditionally made with cachaça, a spirit distilled from fermented sugarcane juice.
