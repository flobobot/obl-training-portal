The Whiskey Sour

Category: Classics

Ingredients

  • 50ml Bourbon
  • 25ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 15ml Sugar Syrup
  • 2 Dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Optional: Egg White

Method

Dry shake (without ice) if using egg white. Then shake with ice and fine strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.

Garnish

Lemon twist or maraschino cherry.

Pro Tip

Use a rich syrup (2:1) for a more velvety finish. Adding egg white creates a silky foam and mellows the acidity.

Did You Know?

The Whiskey Sour dates back to the 1870s and is a foundational sour-style cocktail, balancing spirit, citrus, and sweetness.
