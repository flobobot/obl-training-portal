Classic

Gin Fizz

Ingredients

  • 50ml London Dry Gin
  • 25ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 15ml Sugar Syrup
  • Soda Water to top

Method

Shake first three ingredients with ice, fine strain into a highball or fizz glass filled with ice. Top with soda water. Stir gently to integrate.

Pro Tip

Use chilled soda and pre-chill the glass to keep the fizz strong and refreshing.
