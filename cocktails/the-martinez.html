Classic

The Martinez

Ingredients

  • Hayman’s Dry Gin: 50ml
  • Cocchi Di Torino: 25ml
  • Maraschino: 10ml
  • Angostura Bitters: 5 dashes

Method

Stir with ice until chilled and balanced. Julep strain into a Nick & Nora.

Pro Tip

Think of the Martinez as a bridge between a Manhattan and a Martini — the Maraschino gives it elegant sweetness. Stir with care, if you get a hint of gummy bear you know that you have hit the spot. Some say to add a drop of orange bitters in the ice water when chilling the glass is the key to an outstanding result.
