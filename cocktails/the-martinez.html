Classic

The Martinez

Ingredients

  • Hayman’s Dry Gin: 50ml
  • Cocchi Di Torino: 25ml
  • Maraschino: 10ml
  • Angostura Bitters: 5 dashes

Method

Stir with ice until chilled and balanced. Julep strain into a Nick & Nora.

Glassware

Nick & Nora

Garnish

Lemon twist (+ express and discard orange peel)

Pro Tip

Think of the Martinez as a bridge between a Manhattan and a Martini — the Maraschino gives it elegant sweetness. Stir with care, if you get a hint of gummy bear you know that you have hit the spot. Some say to add a drop of orange bitters in the ice water when chilling the glass is the key to an outstanding result.

Did You Know?

Widely considered the precursor to the Martini, the Martinez dates back to the mid-1800s and showcases how gin-based cocktails evolved. It is a bold and simultaneously fragile masterpiece. It encapsulates everything that a sweet tooth hates, and everything they love - and vice versa. I would go as far as to say that this particular cocktail made me more curious about cocktails in general. En masse, a great way to endure even the most tedious of company. In moderation, a great way to start an evening. Or finish it.
