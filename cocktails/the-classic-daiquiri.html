The Classic Daiquiri

Tag: Classics

Ingredients

  • 60ml White Rum
  • 25ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 15ml Sugar Syrup

Method

Shake all ingredients with ice and fine strain into a chilled coupette or martini glass.

The Daiquiri is named after a beach near Santiago in Cuba. It was a favorite of Ernest Hemingway and JFK.
