Classic

The Paper Plane

Ingredients

  • Bulleit Bourbon: 25ml
  • Aperol: 25ml
  • Amaro Averna: 25ml
  • Lemon Juice: 25ml

Method

Shake hard with ice and fine strain into a Nick & Nora or coupe.

Glassware

Nick & Nora

Garnish

None or lemon peel, expressed
