    <h1>Privateer</h1>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>40ml Spiced Rum</li>

      <li>20ml Cognac</li>

      <li>25ml Lime Juice</li>

      <li>20ml Orgeat</li>

      <li>2 dashes Angostura Bitters</li>

    </ul>

    <h2>Method</h2>

    <p>Shake hard with ice and fine strain into a chilled rocks glass over fresh ice. Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wedge.</p>

    <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

    <p>Use a quality orgeat like Giffard or Small Hands to balance the spice and citrus. Slap the mint before garnishing to release aroma.</p>

    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The term “privateer” refers to state-licensed pirates—fitting for a rum-based drink with bold, adventurous character.</p>
