Classic

Flint

Ingredients

  • Martell: 30ml
  • Green Chartreuse: 5ml
  • Midori: 10ml
  • Pomelo Pink & Pepper Tonic: 40ml

Method

Build over ice in a highball glass. Gently stir to integrate.

Glassware

Highball

Garnish

Grapefruit twist or melon ball

Pro Tip

This is a layered flavour bomb: herbal, fruity, and spicy from the tonic. Serve cold and bright.

Did You Know?

Inspired by the mineral and fruity contrast found in Flint terroirs, this drink plays off the complex mid-tones of chartreuse and melon.
