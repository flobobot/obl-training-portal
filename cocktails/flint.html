House Riff

Flint

Ingredients

  • 30ml Mezcal
  • 15ml Montenegro
  • 10ml Aperol
  • 20ml Lime juice
  • 15ml Agave syrup
  • 2 dash Peychaud's bitters

Method

Shake with ice and fine strain into a chilled coupette.

Pro Tip

Chill your glassware beforehand to preserve the brightness of the citrus.

Did You Know?

This smoky, bitter twist reflects the modern shift toward mezcal and amari in contemporary craft cocktail bars.
