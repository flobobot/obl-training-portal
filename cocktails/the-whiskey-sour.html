Classic

The Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • Woodford Reserve: 50ml
  • Lemon Juice: 25ml
  • Simple Syrup or Gomme: 10ml
  • Egg White: 25ml

Method

Dry shake without ice, then shake again with ice. Fine strain into a chilled coupette.

Pro Tip

Dry shaking helps emulsify egg white for the perfect foam — a hallmark of a well-executed sour.
