Classic

The Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • Woodford Reserve: 50ml
  • Lemon Juice: 25ml
  • Simple Syrup or Gomme: 10ml
  • Egg White: 25ml

Method

Dry shake without ice, then shake again with ice. Fine strain into a chilled coupette.

Glassware

Coupette

Garnish

Angostura design or lemon twist

Did You Know?

One of the oldest cocktail families, the sour template (spirit + citrus + sweet) is found in dozens of drinks across cultures and its simple ratios have become the backbone of modern mixology. Some things will never change. Perfect this drink, present it right and you win the hearts of even the harshest critics.
