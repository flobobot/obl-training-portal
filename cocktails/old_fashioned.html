    <h1>Old Fashioned</h1>

    <ul>

      <li>50ml Bourbon</li>

      <li>5ml Sugar Syrup</li>

      <li>2 dashes Angostura Bitters</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <p>Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well chilled. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube. Garnish with an orange twist.</p>


    <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

    <p>A single large ice cube melts slower and keeps the drink cold without dilution. Stir longer than you think you need to — it's all about dilution and chill.</p>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The Old Fashioned dates back to the early 1800s and is considered one of the first true cocktails. Originally it was just spirits, sugar, water, and bitters.</p>
