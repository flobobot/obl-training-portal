Classic

The Amaretto Sour

Ingredients

  • Disaranno Amaretto: 40ml
  • Lemon Juice: 25ml
  • Sugar Syrup: 5ml
  • Egg White: 20ml
  • Rittenhouse: 20ml

Method

Reverse dry-shake. Shake all ingredients without ice, then shake again with ice. Fine strain into a coupette, though some prefer it on the rocks.

Pro Tip

Adding a bold rye whiskey like Rittenhouse offsets the sweetness of the Amaretto and gives structure.
