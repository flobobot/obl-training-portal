Classic

The Amaretto Sour

Tag: Classics

Ingredients

  • 45ml Amaretto
  • 25ml Lemon Juice
  • 15ml Sugar Syrup
  • 1 dash Angostura Bitters
  • Optional: 15ml Egg White or Aquafaba

Method

Dry shake all ingredients without ice to emulsify. Add ice and shake again until chilled. Strain into a rocks glass filled with fresh ice. Garnish with a cherry or orange slice if desired.

Pro Tip

Egg white or aquafaba adds a rich, velvety texture—don’t skip it if you want the full experience.

Did You Know?

The Amaretto Sour gained fame in the 1970s but has been revived by bartenders with better balance and the addition of bitters and citrus.
