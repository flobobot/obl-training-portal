Classic

In the Woods

Ingredients

  • Cacao Nib infused Stoli: 50ml
  • Lime: 10ml
  • Beetroot & Blackberry Cordial: 20ml
  • Orange Bitters: 2 dashes

Method

Shake hard and fine strain into a chilled coupette.

Pro Tip

Batching the cordial in-house lets you control sugar and acidity — a true Level 3 showcase.
