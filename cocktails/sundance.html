Classic

Sundance

Ingredients

  • Havana 3yo Rum: 30ml
  • Bulleit Rye: 20ml
  • Dolin Sweet Vermouth (red): 20ml
  • Cynar: 5ml
  • Frangelico: 10ml

Method

Stir with ice and strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.

Glassware

Rocks

Garnish

Orange twist

Pro Tip

Balancing rye spice, rum warmth, and bitter liqueurs — a riff on the Connaught’s Everglow.

Did You Know?

Cynar is an artichoke-based amaro, bringing vegetal depth to many contemporary cocktails. In this case the seeming overkill in ingredients really pays off and creates a rare but unique and surprisingly round finish.
