Reverse Adonis

Ingredients

  • Fino Sherry: 20ml
  • Mezcal Verde: 5ml
  • Belzasar Rose: 40ml
  • Orange Bitters: 1 dash

Method

Shake with ice and fine strain into a chilled N&N.

Glassware

Nick & Nora

Garnish

Grapefruit twist
