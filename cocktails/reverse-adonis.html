Reverse Adonis

🍸 Ingredients

  • 20ml Fino Sherry
  • 5ml Mezcal Verde
  • 40ml Belzasar Rosé
  • 1 dash Orange Bitters

🧪 Method

Shake with ice and fine strain into a chilled Nick & Nora.

🥃 Glassware

Nick & Nora

🍊 Garnish

Grapefruit twist

✅ Pro Tip

Shaking highlights the lighter florals of the rosé vermouth and gives the drink a lively texture.

💡 Did You Know?

The original Adonis dates back to the 1880s and was named after a Broadway musical. This version inverts it with Mezcal and rosé vermouth for a modern twist.
