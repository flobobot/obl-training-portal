Classic

Reverse Adonis

Ingredients

  • Fino Sherry: 20ml
  • Mezcal Verde: 5ml
  • Belzasar Rose: 40ml
  • Orange Bitters: 1 dash

Method

Shake with ice and fine strain into a chilled N&N.

Pro Tip

Shaking highlights the lighter florals of the rosé vermouth and gives the drink a lively texture.
