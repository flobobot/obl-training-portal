Mezcal Paloma

[Image Placeholder]

Ingredients

  • 50ml Mezcal
  • 25ml Pink grapefruit juice
  • 15ml Lime juice
  • 10ml Agave syrup
  • Soda water (top)
  • Salt rim (optional)

Method

Shake mezcal, grapefruit juice, lime juice, and agave syrup with ice. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice. Top with soda water. Optional: salt the rim of the glass before pouring.

Pro Tip

Use fresh pink grapefruit juice for brightness, and balance sweetness with the tart lime.

Did You Know?

The Paloma, not the Margarita, is often considered the most popular tequila-based drink in Mexico. This mezcal twist adds a smoky backbone.
