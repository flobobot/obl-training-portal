House Riff

Mezcal Paloma

Ingredients

  • Quiquiriqui Mezcal: 50ml
  • Lime: 20ml
  • Agave: 5ml
  • Grapefruit: 40ml
  • Pomelo Pink & Pepper Tonic or soda: 60ml

Method

Build in a Collins glass over ice, stir gently. Top with tonic (or soda depending on availability).

Glassware

Collins

Garnish

Grapefruit wedge and lime wheel

Pro Tip

Balance the grapefruit and agave — Mezcal’s smoky note can dominate if unbalanced.

Did You Know?

A Paloma is more commonly drunk than a Margarita in Mexico. The mezcal twist adds richness and modern flair.
