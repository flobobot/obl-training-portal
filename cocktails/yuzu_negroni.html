Yuzu Negroni

A Japanese citrus twist on the classic Negroni.
[Image goes here]

Ingredients

  • 25ml Yuzu gin
  • 25ml Campari
  • 25ml Sweet vermouth

Method

Stir ingredients with ice until well chilled. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice.

Pro Tip

Express a yuzu peel over the drink and drop it in for maximum aroma and flair.

Did You Know?

Yuzu is a prized Japanese citrus fruit known for its fragrant and complex flavour. This cocktail is a modern spin on the iconic Negroni using Japanese ingredients.
