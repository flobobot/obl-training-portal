    <h1>Cherry Mezcalita</h1>


    <h2>Ingredients</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>50ml Mezcal (Cazcabel or Quiquiriqui)</li>

      <li>25ml Lime juice</li>

      <li>15ml Cherry syrup</li>

      <li>2 dashes Orange bitters</li>

      <li>Tajin rim</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <p>Shake all ingredients hard with ice. Fine strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice. Rim glass with tajin before pouring.</p>


    <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

    <p>Works best with house-made cherry syrup. Be sure to balance tartness – too much sugar drowns the mezcal.</p>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>This twist on the Mezcalita is perfect for drinkers looking for a fruit-forward smoky number. Surprisingly popular with Negroni fans!</p>
