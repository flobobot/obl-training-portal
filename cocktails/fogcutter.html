Fogcutter

[Image Placeholder]

Ingredients

  • 30ml London Dry Gin
  • 30ml Cognac
  • 15ml White Rum
  • 15ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 15ml Orange Juice
  • 15ml Orgeat Syrup
  • 15ml Cream Sherry (float)

Method

Shake all ingredients except the sherry with ice and strain into a highball glass filled with ice. Float the cream sherry on top. Garnish with an orange slice and mint sprig.

Pro Tip

This tiki-style drink is bold and layered. The sherry float adds complexity and aroma—don't skip it!

Did You Know?

The Fogcutter hails from the golden age of tiki and was a favourite in bars like Trader Vic’s.
