    <h1>Fogcutter</h1>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>45ml White Rum</li>

      <li>15ml Cognac</li>

      <li>15ml London Dry Gin</li>

      <li>30ml Fresh Lemon Juice</li>

      <li>15ml Orange Juice</li>

      <li>15ml Orgeat Syrup</li>

      <li>15ml Cream Sherry (float)</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <ol>

      <li>Shake all ingredients except the sherry with ice.</li>

      <li>Strain into a tall glass filled with crushed ice.</li>

      <li>Float the cream sherry on top using the back of a spoon.</li>

    </ol>


    <h2>Pro Tips</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>Use high-quality orgeat to balance the drink’s acidity and spirit-forward profile.</li>

      <li>The sherry float adds complexity and visual flair—don’t skip it.</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The Fogcutter was a post-Prohibition tiki classic, notorious for its boozy punch. The saying goes: "Fogcutter—after two of these, you won't even see the cutter."</p>
