Classic

Fogcutter

Ingredients

  • Diplomatico: 25ml
  • Greenalls: 15ml
  • Martell VS: 15ml
  • Lemon: 20ml
  • Orange Juice: 20ml
  • Orgeat: 15ml
  • Fino Sherry: 10ml

Method

Shake with ice, strain into a highball glass, float sherry on top.

Glassware

Highball

Garnish

Mint sprig

Pro Tip

Layering the fino on top preserves its crisp dryness before blending with the rich body below.

Did You Know?

The Fogcutter is a tiki classic invented in the 1940s — known as ‘the Long Island Iced Tea of the tropics.’
