The Perfect Manhattan

Ingredients

  • 30ml Rye Whiskey
  • 15ml Sweet Vermouth
  • 15ml Dry Vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura Bitters

Method

Stir all ingredients with ice until well chilled. Strain into a chilled coupette glass. Garnish with a cherry or a lemon twist.

Pro Tips

Use high-quality vermouths and balance the sweetness and dryness to taste. Stir for at least 20 seconds to ensure proper dilution and chill.
