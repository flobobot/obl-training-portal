Classic

Tommy’s Margarita

Category: Classic

Ingredients

  • 50ml Reposado Tequila
  • 25ml Lime Juice
  • 15ml Agave Syrup

Method

Shake all ingredients with ice and fine strain into a rocks glass over ice.

Pro Tip

Use a rich agave syrup (2:1) for more body and texture. You can rim the glass with salt for an added kick.

Did You Know?

Tommy’s Margarita was created by Julio Bermejo in the 1990s at his family's restaurant, Tommy's Mexican Restaurant in San Francisco. It's now considered the modern classic Margarita.
