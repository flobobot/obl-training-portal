Classic

Apricot Gin Fizz

Ingredients

  • Portobello Road Gin: 50ml
  • Apricot Syrup: 12.5ml
  • Lemon Juice: 25ml

Method

Shake with ice and strain into a Collins glass. Top with soda.

Glassware

Collins

Garnish

Lemon slice
