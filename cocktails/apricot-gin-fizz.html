  <h1>Apricot Gin Fizz</h1>


  <h2>Ingredients</h2>

  <ul>

    <li>35ml Apricot-infused Gin</li>

    <li>15ml Lemon Juice</li>

    <li>10ml Sugar Syrup</li>

    <li>Top Soda Water</li>

  </ul>


  <h2>Method</h2>

  <ol>

    <li>Shake gin, lemon juice, and syrup with ice.</li>

    <li>Strain into a highball glass over fresh ice.</li>

    <li>Top with soda water.</li>

    <li>Garnish with lemon wedge or twist.</li>

  </ol>


  <h2>Pro Tips</h2>

  <p>Use clear ice and keep the soda chilled to maintain fizz. This is a vibrant and refreshing serve — ideal as a summer highball or aperitif.</p>


  <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

  <p>The Gin Fizz dates back to the 1870s, but this apricot variation adds a fruity twist that fits a modern palate without losing the elegance of the original.</p>
