Apricot Gin Fizz

Ingredients

  • 50ml Apricot Gin
  • 25ml Lemon Juice
  • 15ml Sugar Syrup
  • Soda Water (to top)

Method

Shake the gin, lemon juice, and sugar syrup with ice. Fine strain into a highball glass filled with ice. Top with soda water and gently stir to integrate.

Pro Tip

Use a high-quality apricot gin to get the fruitiness without artificial aftertaste. A twist of lemon zest adds a sharp aroma on the nose.

Did You Know?

The Fizz is one of the oldest cocktail templates, originating in the late 1800s. This version adds a fruity spin using apricot-infused gin.
