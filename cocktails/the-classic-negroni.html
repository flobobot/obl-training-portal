The Classic Negroni

Category: Classics

Ingredients

  • 25ml London Dry Gin
  • 25ml Campari
  • 25ml Sweet Vermouth

Method

Stir all ingredients with ice until well chilled, then strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube. Garnish with an orange twist.

Pro Tip

Use a quality vermouth and store it in the fridge once opened to maintain freshness.

Did You Know?

The Negroni was reportedly invented in Florence, Italy, when Count Camillo Negroni asked his bartender to strengthen his Americano by adding gin.
