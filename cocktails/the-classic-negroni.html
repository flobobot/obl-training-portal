The Classic Negroni

Ingredients

  • Tanqueray: 25ml
  • Cocchi di Torrino: 25ml
  • Campari: 25ml

Method

Stir well with ice in a Japanese stirring glass for dramatic effect - however this concoction should be “thrown” to aerate and add smoothness to this ever-bold drink. Strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice, ideally one big cube.

Glassware

Rocks

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and stuck to the side, like a rabbit's ear.

Pro Tip

Tanqueray’s bold juniper works beautifully with Cocchi’s bitter-sweet complexity. Stir until the liquid clings slightly to the glass.

Did You Know?

The Negroni originated in Florence around 1919 when Count Camillo Negroni requested his Americano be made stronger. It’s now the world’s go-to aperitivo (or fuel for next-day dread)
