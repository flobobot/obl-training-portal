Tequila Paloma

Ingredients:

  • 50ml Tequila
  • 20ml Lime Juice
  • 10ml Sugar Syrup
  • Top with Grapefruit Soda

Method: Build in a highball glass over ice. Top with grapefruit soda and gently stir.

Glassware: Highball

Pro Tip: Salt the rim for a classic touch and to balance bitterness from grapefruit.

Did You Know? The Paloma is considered the most popular tequila-based cocktail in Mexico, often enjoyed as a refreshing alternative to the Margarita.
