Classic

Tequila Paloma

Ingredients

  • Cazcabel Reposado: 50ml
  • Lime: 20ml
  • Agave: 5ml
  • Grapefruit: 40ml
  • Pomelo Pink & Pepper Tonic or soda: 60ml

Method

Build in a highball with ice, stir, and top with tonic or soda.

Pro Tip

Add a pinch of salt to highlight the peppery reposado and citrus.
