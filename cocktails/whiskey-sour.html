    <h1>Whiskey Sour</h1>


    <h2>Ingredients</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>50ml Bourbon (Buffalo Trace)</li>

      <li>25ml Fresh lemon juice</li>

      <li>15ml Sugar syrup (1:1)</li>

      <li>Optional: Egg white (15ml)</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <p>Dry shake all ingredients (without ice) if using egg white. Add ice and shake again. Fine strain into a rocks glass with fresh ice or a coupette if served up.</p>


    <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

    <p>For extra froth, reverse dry shake. Use large ice for a slower dilution rate and a more consistent sip.</p>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The Whiskey Sour was first documented in the 1860s, making it one of the oldest recorded cocktails still served today.</p>
