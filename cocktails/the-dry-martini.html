The Dry Martini

Category: Classics

Ingredients

  • 60ml London Dry Gin
  • 10ml Dry Vermouth
  • 1 dash Orange Bitters (optional)

Method

Stir all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or olive.

Did You Know?

The Dry Martini is a symbol of sophistication and minimalism. Its origins trace back to the early 20th century, evolving into a classic cocktail known for its elegance and simplicity.
