Classic

The Dry Martini

Ingredients

  • East Ldn Vodka or Gin: 50ml
  • Dollin White Vermouth: 10ml

Method

Stir over ice until well chilled, ideally reaching sub-zero temperature. Julep strain from hight into a chilled Nick & Nora or Martini glass. Always ask guest for their spirit preference (vodka or gin).

Glassware

Nick & Nora

Garnish

Lemon twist or olive (guest choice)

Pro Tip

Using quality ice and a proper chill on the glass makes a huge difference in clarity and texture.
