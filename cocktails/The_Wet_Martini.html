The Wet Martini

Category: Classics

Glassware: Martini glass

Ingredients

  • 50ml London Dry Gin
  • 25ml Dry Vermouth
  • 1 dash Orange Bitters (optional)

Method

Stir all ingredients with ice in a mixing glass until well chilled. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or an olive.

Pro Tip

For a 'wetter' martini, increase the vermouth ratio. This version is more balanced than the bone-dry classic and highlights the botanical harmony.

Did You Know?

The term “wet” refers to the amount of vermouth — classic martinis are often dry with very little vermouth. This version gives vermouth its proper stage.
