Classic

The Wet Martini

Ingredients

  • East Ldn Vodka or Gin: 50ml
  • Dollin White Vermouth: 25ml

Method

Stir thoroughly with plenty of ice until well chilled and slightly diluted. Strain into a chilled Nick & Nora.

Pro Tip

A ‘wet’ Martini uses a higher proportion of vermouth. It’s smoother, more aromatic, and more approachable for Martini newcomers.
