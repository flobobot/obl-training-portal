Classic

The Wet Martini

Ingredients

  • East Ldn Vodka or Gin: 50ml
  • Dollin White Vermouth: 25ml

Method

Stir thoroughly with plenty of ice until well chilled and slightly diluted. Strain into a chilled Nick & Nora.

Glassware

Nick & Nora

Garnish

Lemon twist or olive

Pro Tip

A ‘wet’ Martini uses a higher proportion of vermouth. It’s smoother, more aromatic, and more approachable for Martini newcomers.

Did You Know?

Winston Churchill famously preferred his Martini bone dry — but the wet version is now making a comeback as palates evolve. The difference is subtle, it’s still booze stirred with booze. Some may even say that … how did I get this drunk? I have seen battle hardened drinkers meet the creator with this surprisingly smooth concoction. Like most Martinis, the liquid should be chilled to sub-zero, which makes the pour a true experience. The viscosity of the liquid changes and you will feel like a true alchemist if done right.
