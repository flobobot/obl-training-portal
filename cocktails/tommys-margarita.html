Classic

Tommy’s Margarita

Ingredients

  • Ocho 8 Tequila: 50ml
  • Lime Juice: 25ml
  • Agave Syrup: 20ml

Method

Shake vigorously with ice. Fine strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.

Glassware

Rocks

Garnish

Dehydrated lime wheel or expressed lime zest

Pro Tip

Use fresh lime juice — bottled alternatives dull the agave’s natural brightness. Shake hard for aeration.

Did You Know?

Tommy’s Margarita replaces triple sec with agave syrup, invented at Tommy’s Mexican Restaurant in San Francisco — it’s now the global gold standard for a clean Margarita.
