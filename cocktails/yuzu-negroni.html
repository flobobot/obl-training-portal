    <h1>Yuzu Negroni</h1>


    <h2>Ingredients</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>25ml Gin (Beefeater or Ki No Bi)</li>

      <li>25ml Campari</li>

      <li>25ml Yuzu liqueur</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <p>Stir all ingredients with ice until well chilled. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube. Garnish with a twist of grapefruit or a thin slice of dehydrated citrus.</p>


    <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

    <p>Chill the glass beforehand and use quality ice to prevent over-dilution. Yuzu can vary in intensity—adjust to taste.</p>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The Yuzu Negroni adds a Japanese twist to the classic Italian aperitivo, balancing bitter and citrus in an elegant profile.</p>
