Yuzu Negroni

Ingredients

  • Portobello Road Gin: 25ml
  • Punt e Mes: 15ml
  • Choya Yuzu: 25ml
  • Campari: 25ml

Method

Stir over ice until chilled. Strain over fresh ice into a rocks glass.

Pro Tip

Choya Yuzu adds a soft, floral-citrus profile that lifts the bitterness of Campari.
