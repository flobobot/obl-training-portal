  <h1>Fig Negroni</h1>

  <pre>

25ml Gin  

25ml Campari  

25ml Fig-infused Sweet Vermouth  


Method: Stir with ice and strain into rocks glass over large ice cube. Garnish with fig slice or orange twist.


Pro Tip: Let the fig vermouth infuse for at least 24 hours for rich depth without overwhelming sweetness.


Did You Know? The Negroni, invented in Florence, was originally a stronger version of the Americano – Count Negroni requested gin in place of soda water.
