    <h1>Classic Daiquiri</h1>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>60ml White Rum</li>

      <li>25ml Fresh Lime Juice</li>

      <li>15ml Sugar Syrup (1:1)</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <ol>

      <li>Shake all ingredients with ice until well chilled.</li>

      <li>Double strain into a chilled coupette.</li>

      <li>No garnish necessary—let the drink speak for itself.</li>

    </ol>


    <h2>Pro Tips</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>Balance is key. Adjust the syrup slightly to taste, especially with different rum brands.</li>

      <li>Use fresh lime juice only—no bottled juice here.</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The Daiquiri was a favourite of Ernest Hemingway, although he preferred his extra dry and served frozen. This classic version is the original—and arguably the best.</p>
