Classic Daiquiri

Ingredients

  • Planteray 3yo Rum: 50ml
  • Lime Juice: 20ml
  • Sugar Syrup: 15ml

Method

Shake hard with ice and fine strain into a chilled coupette or Nick & Nora.

Pro Tip

Balance is everything: the sugar and lime should elevate the rum, not hide it. Adjust ratios slightly for guest preference.
