    <h1>White Lady</h1>

    <ul>

      <li>40ml Gin</li>

      <li>25ml Triple Sec (Cointreau)</li>

      <li>25ml Fresh Lemon Juice</li>

      <li>10ml Sugar Syrup</li>

      <li>Optional: Egg White</li>

    </ul>

    <h2>Method</h2>

    <p>Dry shake all ingredients if using egg white, then shake again with ice. Fine strain into a chilled coupette glass.</p>

    <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

    <p>Use fresh lemon juice and a quality triple sec like Cointreau for the best balance of citrus and sweetness.</p>

    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The White Lady was reportedly created by Harry MacElhone at Harry’s New York Bar in Paris. Originally made with crème de menthe, it evolved into this sharper gin-based version.</p>
