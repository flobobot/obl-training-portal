    <h1>Mezcalita</h1>


    <h2>Ingredients</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>50ml Mezcal (Quiquiriqui)</li>

      <li>25ml Lime juice</li>

      <li>15ml Agave syrup</li>

      <li>3 dashes Orange bitters</li>

      <li>Tajin rim</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <p>Shake all ingredients hard with ice. Fine strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice. Rim glass with tajin before pouring.</p>


    <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

    <p>Using fresh lime juice is critical — anything else kills the smoke. You can batch this one, but shake to order for max aroma.</p>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The Mezcalita is a smoky cousin of the Margarita, beloved for its earthy backbone and rising popularity in modern craft bars.</p>
