Mezcalita

Ingredients

  • Quiquiriqui Matatlan Mezcal: 50ml
  • Simple Syrup: 15ml
  • Grapefruit Juice: 25ml
  • Cointreau Triple Sec: 10ml

Method

Shake and fine strain into a rocks glass over ice.

Pro Tip

Mezcal and grapefruit are natural partners — keep the syrup light to let the spirit shine.
