Negroni Sbagliato

Ingredients

  • 25ml Campari
  • 25ml Sweet Vermouth
  • Top with Prosecco

Method

Build in a chilled rocks glass over cubed ice. Stir gently. Garnish with an orange wedge or twist.

Pro Tip

Use a decent quality Prosecco—brut styles work best to balance the Campari’s bitterness.

Did You Know?

“Sbagliato” means “mistaken” in Italian. The story goes that a bartender accidentally used sparkling wine instead of gin—and created this now-iconic bubbly twist.
