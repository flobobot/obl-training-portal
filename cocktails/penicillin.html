    <h1>Penicillin</h1>

    <ul>

      <li>50ml Blended Scotch Whisky</li>

      <li>20ml Fresh Lemon Juice</li>

      <li>15ml Honey-Ginger Syrup</li>

      <li>10ml Islay Single Malt (float)</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <p>Shake all ingredients except the Islay whisky with ice and strain over ice into a rocks glass. Float the Islay on top. Garnish with candied ginger or a lemon slice.</p>


    <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

    <p>You can batch the honey-ginger syrup: equal parts honey and water, simmered with fresh ginger and strained. The float of peaty whisky gives the signature smoky top note.</p>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>Created by Sam Ross (again!) at Milk & Honey, this is the cocktail equivalent of a hot toddy — comforting and complex, with a medicinal twist.</p>
