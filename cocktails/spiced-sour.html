Spiced Sour

Type: Alcohol-free

Ingredients

  • 50ml Seedlip Spice 94
  • 25ml Lemon Juice
  • 20ml Sugar Syrup
  • Dash of Bitters
  • Aquafaba or Egg White (optional)

Method

Shake all ingredients with ice. Fine strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass over fresh ice. Garnish with a lemon twist or dehydrated citrus wheel.
