Non-Alcoholic

Spiced Sour

Ingredients

  • Seedlip Garden: 50ml
  • Simple Syrup or Gomme: 15ml
  • Lemon: 20ml
  • Egg: 20ml

Method

Dry shake, then shake again with ice. Fine strain into a coupette.

Glassware

Coupette

Garnish

Nutmeg dust or lemon twist

Pro Tip

Great non-alcoholic option with all the ceremony of a classic sour. Texture is everything.
