  <h1>Classic Daiquiri</h1>

  <pre>

50ml White Rum

25ml Fresh Lime Juice

15ml Sugar Syrup


Method: Shake with ice and fine strain into a chilled coupette.


Pro Tip: Avoid the temptation to over-sweeten. A good Daiquiri should be bright, dry and razor-sharp.


Did You Know? Hemingway's variation, the “Papa Doble,” doubled the rum and removed the sugar entirely.
