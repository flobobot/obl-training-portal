Classic

Negroni Spagliato

Ingredients

  • Cocchi Di Torino: 25ml
  • Campari: 25ml
  • Prosecco: 50ml

Method

Build in a wine glass or rocks glass over ice and lift gently with a bar spoon.

Glassware

Wine or Rocks

Garnish

Orange wedge or peel

Pro Tip

Great for Negroni lovers seeking a lighter, sparkling alternative.

Did You Know?

The name means ‘mistaken’ in Italian — a bartender accidentally swapped gin for Prosecco, and history was made. One of the most iconic mistakes ever made and one of the few ones we have very little to complain about. It makes an otherwise bold “drinker’s drink’ somewhat sophisticated.
