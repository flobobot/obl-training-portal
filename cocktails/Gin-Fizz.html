  <h1>Gin Fizz</h1>

  <pre>

50ml Gin  

25ml Lemon Juice  

15ml Sugar Syrup  

Egg White or Aquafaba (optional)  

Top with Soda Water  

Garnish: Lemon Slice or Zest


Method: Shake all ingredients (except soda) with ice. Fine strain into a highball or Collins glass, top with soda.


Pro Tip: Want that classic froth? Shake without ice first, then again with—then strain. Add soda last and gently stir.


Did You Know? The Gin Fizz dates back to the late 1800s and is the fizzy sibling of the sour family.
