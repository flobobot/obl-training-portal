Classic

The Paper Plane

Classics

Ingredients

  • 25ml Bourbon
  • 25ml Aperol
  • 25ml Amaro (such as Montenegro)
  • 25ml Fresh Lemon Juice

Method

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled coupe glass. No garnish needed.

Pro Tips

  • Balance is key—this cocktail lives and dies by its proportions.
  • Fresh lemon juice is essential—don't sub with bottled.

Did You Know?

Created by Sam Ross (of Milk & Honey) in 2007, the Paper Plane is named after the M.I.A. song and has become a modern classic.
