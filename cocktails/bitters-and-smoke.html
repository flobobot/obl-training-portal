# Bitters & Smoke

### 🍸 Ingredients

- Cazcabel Reposado: 25ml
- Cynar: 25ml
- Fernet Branca: 12.5ml
- Mezcal Verde: 12.5ml
- Lime: 20ml

### 🧪 Method

Stir in mixing glass, strain into a chilled Nick & Nora

### 🥃 Glassware

Nick & Nora

### 🍊 Garnish

Grapefruit twist

### ✅ Pro Tip

Stirring preserves the integrity of bitters and brings out complexity in smoky elements.

### 💡 Did You Know?

This modern bitter-sour hybrid balances amaro richness with mezcal’s earthiness — great for adventurous palates.
