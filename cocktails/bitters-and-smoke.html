    <h1>Bitters & Smoke</h1>


    <section>

      <h2>Ingredients</h2>

      <ul>

        <li>35ml Mezcal</li>

        <li>15ml Amaro Montenegro</li>

        <li>10ml Cynar</li>

        <li>2 dashes Orange Bitters</li>

        <li>Orange peel (garnish)</li>

      </ul>

    </section>


    <section>

      <h2>Method</h2>

      <p>Stir all ingredients with ice until well-chilled. Strain into a rocks glass over a large cube. Express orange peel over the top and garnish with the peel.</p>

    </section>


    <section>

      <h2>Pro Tips</h2>

      <ul>

        <li>Use a block of clear ice for maximum visual impact and slow dilution.</li>

        <li>This is a great recommendation for Negroni lovers looking for something darker and smokier.</li>

      </ul>

    </section>


    <section>

      <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

      <p>This smoky, amaro-forward number offers layered complexity while staying sessionable. Inspired by late-night kitchen drinks at The Old Blue Last.</p>
