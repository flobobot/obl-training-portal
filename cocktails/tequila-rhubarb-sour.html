Tequila Rhubarb Sour

Ingredients:

  • 50ml Tequila (Blanco or Reposado)
  • 25ml Rhubarb Syrup
  • 25ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1 Egg White (or aquafaba)
  • Optional: Dash of Rhubarb Bitters

Method:

  1. Dry shake all ingredients (no ice) to emulsify.
  2. Shake again with ice until well chilled.
  3. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass.

Pro Tip: Garnish with a thin rhubarb ribbon or a few drops of bitters for visual flair.

Did You Know? Rhubarb has been used as a medicinal plant since ancient Chinese times, but in cocktails it lends a unique tart-sweet complexity.
