House Riff

Tequila Rhubarb Sour

Ingredients

  • Cazcabel Blanco: 50ml
  • Lemon Juice: 20ml
  • Rhubarb & Strawberry Cordial: 20ml
  • Egg White: 20ml

Method

Reverse dry shake and fine strain into a chilled coupette.

Glassware

Coupette

Garnish

Dash of Angostura on top

Pro Tip

Reverse dry shake ensures maximum foam and tight texture for egg white sours.
