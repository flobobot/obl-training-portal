    <h1>Mezcal Old Fashioned</h1>

    <ul>

      <li>50ml Mezcal</li>

      <li>5ml Agave syrup (or simple syrup)</li>

      <li>2 dashes Angostura bitters</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <p>Stir all ingredients with ice in a mixing glass. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube.</p>


    <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

    <p>Smoke-forward mezcal works best — don't go too sweet. Express an orange peel over the drink and drop it in.</p>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>This riff on the Old Fashioned swaps whiskey for mezcal, adding a smoky, earthy punch to the classic formula. A bartender favourite when looking to twist something timeless.</p>
