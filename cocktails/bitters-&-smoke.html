Classic

Bitters & Smoke

Ingredients

  • Cazcabel Reposado: 25ml
  • Cynar: 25ml
  • Fernet Branca: 12.5ml
  • Mezcal Verde: 12.5ml
  • Lime: 20ml

Method

Stir in mixing glass, strain into a chilled Nick & Nora

Pro Tip

Stirring preserves the integrity of bitters and brings out complexity in smoky elements.
