  <h1>In The Woods</h1>

  <pre>

30ml Rye Whiskey  

30ml Punt e Mes  

15ml Fernet Branca  

2 dashes Angostura Bitters  

Garnish: Orange Zest


Method: Stir all ingredients over ice. Strain into rocks glass over a large cube.


Pro Tip: This is not for the faint-hearted. Don’t over-dilute—just chill and hit it quick.


Did You Know? Inspired by forest walks and bitter truths, this drink was crafted as an earthy alternative to your standard Manhattan.
