In the Woods

[Image Placeholder]

Ingredients

  • 40ml Rye Whiskey
  • 10ml Fernet Branca
  • 10ml Maple Syrup
  • 2 Dashes Chocolate Bitters

Method

Stir all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled rocks glass over fresh ice. Garnish with a twist of orange or a cherry.

Pro Tip

This drink benefits from a rich maple syrup—avoid artificial alternatives. Stir well to mellow the Fernet’s bitterness into the background.

Did You Know?

“In the Woods” riffs on the Manhattan template, grounding it with earthy maple and the forest-floor bitterness of Fernet.
