    <h1>French Rubi</h1>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>40ml Lillet Rosé</li>

      <li>10ml Rhubarb Syrup</li>

      <li>Top with Tonic Water</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <ol>

      <li>Build all ingredients in a highball glass over ice.</li>

      <li>Gently stir to combine.</li>

      <li>Garnish with a grapefruit wedge or fresh rhubarb stick if available.</li>

    </ol>


    <h2>Pro Tips</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>Keep Lillet chilled before serving for optimal freshness.</li>

      <li>This is a perfect low-ABV option—great for aperitif menus.</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The French Rubi is a light, floral spritz-style drink showcasing the subtle elegance of Lillet Rosé and seasonal rhubarb. A contemporary classic born from simplicity.</p>
