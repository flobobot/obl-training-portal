French Rubi

Ingredients

  • 40ml Lillet Blanc
  • 20ml Pink Grapefruit Juice
  • 10ml Rhubarb Syrup
  • Top with Soda

Method

Build all ingredients over ice in a wine glass. Stir gently to combine and garnish.

Glassware

Wine Glass

Garnish

Grapefruit Slice
