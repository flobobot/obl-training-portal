House Riff

French Rubi

Ingredients

  • Martell VS: 25ml
  • Rhubarb Cordial: 25ml
  • Pastis: 10ml
  • Prosecco: 60ml

Method

Build in a wine glass or flute. Stir gently to integrate.

Glassware

Flute or large wine glass

Garnish

Rhubarb ribbon or mint sprig suggested

Pro Tip

If prosecco is too fizzy, stir gently or use a dash of soda to balance. This drink’s success lies in flavour harmony — not sparkle.

Did You Know?

Pastis (aniseed liqueur) and rhubarb are rarely paired — this high-concept drink highlights flavour over fizz, making it ideal for staff R&D or special menu drops.
