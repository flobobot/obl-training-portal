    <h1>Wet Martini</h1>

    <p><strong>Glass:</strong> Nick & Nora</p>

    <p><strong>Ingredients:</strong></p>

    <ul>

      <li>60ml London Dry Gin or Vodka</li>

      <li>15ml Dry Vermouth</li>

      <li>Lemon twist or olive</li>

    </ul>

    <p><strong>Method:</strong> Stir all ingredients over ice until well chilled. Strain into a chilled Nick & Nora glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or olive.</p>

    <p><strong>Pro Tip:</strong> Chill your glass beforehand and don’t skimp on the stir time – clarity and temperature are everything.</p>

    <p><strong>Did You Know?</strong> A 'wet' Martini has a higher proportion of vermouth than the dry version. The term dates back to the golden age of cocktails, when balance mattered more than bravado.</p>
