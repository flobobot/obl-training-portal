Fig Negroni

[Image Placeholder]

Ingredients

  • 25ml Portobello Gin
  • 25ml Fig Infused Campari
  • 25ml Martini Rubino

Method

Stir ingredients with ice and strain into a rocks glass over cubed ice. Garnish with a dehydrated fig or orange twist.

Pro Tip

If you can't find fig-infused Campari, you can infuse it yourself by adding dried figs to a sealed container with Campari and letting it sit for 48 hours.

Did You Know?

This is a seasonal twist on the classic Negroni, with the fig bringing a richer, fruitier tone. Popular in colder months or as a festive feature.
