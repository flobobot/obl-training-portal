Classic

Fig Negroni

Ingredients

  • Tanqueray: 25ml
  • Campari: 25ml
  • Briolette Figue: 5ml
  • Cocchi: 20ml
  • Cacao Bitters: 2ml

Method

Stir with care over ice. Serve on fresh ice in a rocks glass.

Glassware

Rocks

Garnish

Dehydrated fig (if available) or orange peel

Pro Tip

Balance is key — the fig liqueur must accent, not overwhelm. Tanqueray’s punchy gin base holds up well.

Did You Know?

Considered a riff on the Negroni, the fig and cocoa combo adds deep complexity. Ideal for guests seeking something adventurous but familiar.
