Classic

Royal Kir

Ingredients

  • Prosecco: 125ml
  • Hayman’s Sloe Gin: 25ml

Method

Build in a flute. Add sloe gin first, then top with chilled prosecco.

Glassware

Flute

Garnish

Optional lemon twist

Pro Tip

Pour prosecco slowly down the inside of the flute to preserve bubbles.

Did You Know?

The Royal Kir is a sparkling version of the traditional Kir cocktail, which uses crème de cassis and white wine.
