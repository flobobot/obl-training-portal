Royal Kir

Ingredients

  • 10ml Crème de Cassis
  • Top with Sparkling Wine (e.g., Prosecco)

Method

Pour the Crème de Cassis into a chilled flute. Gently top with sparkling wine.

Pro Tip

Make sure both ingredients are well chilled to preserve the bubbles and balance the sweetness.

Did You Know?

The Royal Kir is a sparkling twist on the Kir cocktail, originally named after Félix Kir, a French resistance hero and mayor of Dijon.
