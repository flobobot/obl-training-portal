House Riff

Disco Sour

Ingredients

  • 50ml Whiskey (Buffalo Trace)
  • 25ml Lemon Juice
  • 20ml Sugar Syrup
  • 20ml Pineapple Juice
  • 15ml Red Wine (float)

Method

  1. Add all ingredients except red wine into a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake hard and strain into a coupette.
  3. Carefully float red wine on top using the back of a bar spoon.
