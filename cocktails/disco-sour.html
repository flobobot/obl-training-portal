Disco Sour

Ingredients

  • El Gobernador (apricot infused): 40ml
  • Lemon Juice: 20ml
  • Velvet Falernum: 20ml
  • Benedictine: 10ml
  • Egg White: 25ml
  • Sugar Syrup: 5ml
  • Orange Bitters: 1 dash

Method

Dry shake, then wet shake. Fine strain into a coupette.

Pro Tip

The blend of Falernum, Benedictine and apricot-infused pisco gives this a velvety body and tropical funk. Use with confidence once staff reach Level 3.
