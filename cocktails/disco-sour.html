  <h1>Disco Sour</h1>

  <h2>Ingredients</h2>

  <ul>

    <li>50ml Bourbon</li>

    <li>25ml Lemon Juice</li>

    <li>20ml Cherry Syrup (Luxardo or similar)</li>

    <li>2 dashes Angostura Bitters</li>

    <li>Egg White</li>

  </ul>

  <h2>Method</h2>

  <p>Dry shake all ingredients without ice. Then add ice and shake again hard. Double strain into a coupette glass.</p>

  <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

  <p>For extra foam, let it rest 10 seconds before straining. A drop of saline solution balances the sweetness nicely.</p>

  <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

  <p>Think of it as a party-forward twist on the Whiskey Sour — the cherry element adds colour and flair without going full-on Tiki.</p>
