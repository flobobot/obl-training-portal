Miso Butter Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • Miso Infused Monkey Shoulder: 20ml
  • Brown Sugar Syrup: 15ml
  • Ango bitters: 2 dashes
  • Orange bitters: 1 dash
  • Rittenhouse: 30ml

Method

Stir thoroughly over ice and strain into a rocks glass with large ice cube.

Pro Tip

Miso infusion adds umami — not for everyone, but bold and rich. Stir gently to preserve aromatics.
