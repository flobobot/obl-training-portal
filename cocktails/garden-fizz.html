Classic

Garden Fizz

Ingredients

  • Seedlip Garden: 50ml
  • Lemon Juice: 20ml
  • Elderflower Syrup: 15ml

Method

Shake with ice and strain into a highball or Collins. Top with soda.

Pro Tip

Pre-chill glassware and shake hard to make up for the lack of alcohol “burn” — this keeps the texture crisp and lively.
