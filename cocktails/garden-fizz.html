Garden Fizz

Ingredients:

  • 50ml Gin
  • 15ml Elderflower cordial
  • 25ml Lemon juice
  • Top with soda
  • Cucumber ribbon
  • Mint sprig

Method:

Build all ingredients in a highball glass with cubed ice. Stir gently and top with soda. Garnish with a cucumber ribbon and a mint sprig.

Pro Tip: Slap the mint before garnishing to release its aroma.
