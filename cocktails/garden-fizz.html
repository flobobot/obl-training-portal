Non-Alcoholic

Garden Fizz

Ingredients

  • Seedlip Garden: 50ml
  • Lemon Juice: 20ml
  • Elderflower Syrup: 15ml

Method

Shake with ice and strain into a highball or Collins. Top with soda.

Glassware

Highball

Garnish

Cucumber ribbon or mint sprig

Pro Tip

Pre-chill glassware and shake hard to make up for the lack of alcohol “burn” — this keeps the texture crisp and lively.

Did You Know?

Seedlip was the first non-alcoholic spirit brand to reach global acclaim. Garden 108 uses peas and herbs as base botanicals — a bartender’s friend for N/A menus.
