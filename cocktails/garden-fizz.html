    <h1>Garden Fizz</h1>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>40ml Seedlip Garden 108</li>

      <li>20ml Rhubarb Syrup</li>

      <li>25ml Lemon Juice</li>

      <li>Top with Soda Water</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <ol>

      <li>Shake Seedlip, rhubarb syrup, and lemon juice with ice.</li>

      <li>Strain into a highball glass filled with fresh ice.</li>

      <li>Top with soda and give a gentle stir.</li>

      <li>Garnish with cucumber ribbon or mint sprig.</li>

    </ol>


    <h2>Pro Tips</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>This is a non-alcoholic cocktail—treat it with the same care and flair as any classic serve.</li>

      <li>Use a carbonated soda siphon if available for a fresher fizz.</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The Garden Fizz is a zero-proof cocktail built around Seedlip Garden, capturing the earthy essence of peas, hay, and herbs. A refreshing alternative that doesn't sacrifice complexity.</p>
