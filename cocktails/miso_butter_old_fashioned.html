Miso Butter Old Fashioned

[Insert Image Here]

Ingredients

  • 50ml Buttered Buffalo Trace
  • 5ml Miso Maple
  • 3 Dashes Chocolate Bitters

Method

Stir all ingredients over ice in a mixing glass. Strain into rocks glass over block ice.

Pro Tip

Fat-washing the bourbon with butter gives a rich, velvety body. Use clarified butter for a cleaner finish.

Did You Know?

Miso and maple have both umami and sweet profiles, enhancing complexity and making this drink feel like a dessert Old Fashioned.
