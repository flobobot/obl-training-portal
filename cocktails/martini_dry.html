  <h1>Martini (Dry)</h1>

  <h2>Ingredients:</h2>

  <ul>

    <li>60ml Gin</li>

    <li>10ml Dry Vermouth (e.g., Noilly Prat)</li>

  </ul>


  <h2>Method:</h2>

  <p>Stir all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or an olive, depending on preference.</p>


  <h2>Pro Tip:</h2>

  <p>Always stir, never shake a dry martini—clarity and texture are key. Chill your glass beforehand for the cleanest serve.</p>


  <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

  <p>Winston Churchill was said to make his martinis so dry that he merely looked at the vermouth bottle across the room while pouring the gin.</p>
