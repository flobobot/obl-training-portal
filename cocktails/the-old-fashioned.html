Classic

The Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • Bulleit Bourbon: 50ml
  • Brown Sugar Syrup: 15ml
  • Ango bitters: 2 dash
  • Orange bitters: 1 dash

Method

Stir thoroughly with ice and strain over a single large cube in a rocks glass.

Pro Tip

Use cold, dense ice to control dilution. Brown sugar syrup adds rich depth vs. white, squeezing the peel over the drink will release its oils and therefore a distinct aroma into the drink, some gently wipe the rim of the glass with it.
