35ml Mezcal

20ml Sweet Vermouth

5ml Campari

3 dashes Aromatic Bitters


Method: Stirred over ice, strained into rocks glass with big cube.


Pro Tip: A stirred, mezcal-forward variation on a Negroni. Don't overshake — it’ll dull the smoky bite.


Did You Know? The earthy backbone of mezcal stands up to bold ingredients like bitters and vermouth better than most spirits.
