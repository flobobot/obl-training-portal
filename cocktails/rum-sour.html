    <h1>Rum Sour</h1>

    <ul>

      <li>50ml Dark Rum</li>

      <li>25ml Fresh Lime Juice</li>

      <li>20ml Sugar Syrup (1:1)</li>

      <li>1 Egg White (optional)</li>

      <li>2 dashes Angostura Bitters</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Method</h2>

    <p>Shake all ingredients without ice (if using egg white), then shake again with ice. Double strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice. Garnish with a lime wheel or grated nutmeg.</p>


    <h2>Pro Tip</h2>

    <p>Use a rich, aged rum for depth and complexity. Spiced rums can add a wintery twist. Adjust syrup to taste depending on rum sweetness.</p>


    <h2>Did You Know?</h2>

    <p>The Rum Sour is a descendant of the classic Daiquiri, evolving through tiki culture and sailor traditions into a moodier, darker sibling.</p>
