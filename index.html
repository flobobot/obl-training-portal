Whiskey Sour

50ml Bourbon, 25ml Lemon Juice, 20ml Sugar Syrup, Egg White (optional)

Method: Dry shake, then shake with ice. Strain into rocks glass.

Wet Martini

60ml Gin, 15ml Dry Vermouth

Method: Stirred over ice, strain into a chilled martini glass.
